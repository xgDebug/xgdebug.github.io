6: Affirmative Action, Family Style 6：平权行动，家庭风格#

Highlight(yellow) - Location 5331
THEIR ADULT CHILDREN ARE ECONOMICALLY SELF-SUFFICIENT.他们的成年子女在经济上是自给自足的。
Highlight(yellow) - Location 6104

  1. Never tell children that their parents are wealthy.
  2. 永远不要告诉孩子他们的父母很有钱。
  3. No matter how wealthy you are, teach your children discipline and frugality.
  4. 不管你有多富有，都要教你的孩子纪律和节俭。
  5. Assure that your children won’t realize you’re affluent until after they have established a mature, disciplined, and adult lifestyle and profession.
  6. 确保您的孩子在建立成熟、自律和成人的生活方式和职业之前不会意识到您很富裕。
  7. Minimize discussions of the items that each child and grandchild will inherit or receive as gifts.
  8. 尽量减少讨论每个孩子和孙子将继承或作为礼物收到的物品。
  9. Never give cash or other significant gifts to your adult children as part of a negotiation strategy.
  10. 切勿将现金或其他重要礼物作为谈判策略的一部分送给您的成年子女。
  11. Stay out of your adult children’s family matters.
  12. 远离成年子女的家庭事务。
  13. Don’t try to compete with your children.
  14. 不要试图与你的孩子竞争。 Never boast about how much money you have accumulated.
  15. Always remember that your children are individuals.
  16. 永远记住，你的孩子是独立的个体。
  17. Emphasize your children’s achievements, no matter how small, not their or your symbols of success.
  18. 强调孩子的成就，无论多么渺小，而不是他们或你成功的象征。
  19. . Tell your children that there are a lot of things more valuable than money.
  20. .告诉孩子，有很多东西比金钱更有价值。
